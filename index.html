About

Musician and software engineer based in San Francisco, CA. Passionate about building products that are useful for everyone.

It's very nice to meet you. I graduated from San Francisco State University with a Bachelor's in Mathematics. Living in San Francisco has let me apply my creative side while surrounded myself with artists in different mediums. During my time in college, I found that a career in software development would allow me to exercise my critical thinking skills, while still allowing me to create. Since then, I have developed applications using my favorite frameworks like Rails, React.js, and React Native. I program in Javascript and Ruby, and am always ready to learn new technologies/languages. Check out my work. Feel free to connect with me through LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, or email here.

Projects

expressr.

expressr is a full stack web application built with Ruby on Rails, React and Redux, and uses a PostgreSQL database. expressr was influenced by the social networking site Tumblr, and draws inspiration from social media sites that focus on user content. expressr features a minimal, distraction-free design, allowing users to focus on posts shared by others. Users can post their self expressions by text, or photo. Users can also follow other users, and like their posts.

HueCanvas

HueCanvas is an iPad application that enables users to control their lighting environments with the Philips Hue smart lighting system. This is achieved through an intuitive, gesture-based UI that is malleable and space oriented.

Chromestesia

Chromestesia is a simple to use, single page application designed for musicians. It is a songwriting tool, inspired by the metronome, that uses audio and visuals to help musicians maintain timing and tempo. The colors selected are used by the musician to maintain a certain theme while composing a song.
